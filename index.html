Latin America

Born and raised in the Texas Panhandle with Cherokee heritage, I grew up with a fascination with how Native American and European cultures clashed during the colonization of the Americas. Some of my most nostalgic childhood memories are the times I spent in the plaza in Santa Fe, New Mexico. This inclination has drawn me to study the rich history of Latin America. In the past three years, we've explored Mexico, and we have trips planned for Central and South America in the near future.
