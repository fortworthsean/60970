Fighting Back: Native Resistance to Colonial Authority

Introduction

A culturally rich and diverse region today, Latin America became a cultural battleground in the late 15th century. Colonial authorities sought to control and subjugate native populations by enforcing strict controls on government, urban development, society, and economic activity. However, Native Americans were not passive.
